How to Use a 15 Second Timer

The Power of 15 Seconds

15 seconds may seem brief, but it is a scientifically significant interval in health and fitness. Dental professionals worldwide recommend the quadrant brushing method: divide your mouth into four sections and spend 15-30 seconds on each for optimal oral hygiene.

In exercise science, 15-second intervals are commonly used in Tabata-style training and HIIT workouts. This duration engages fast-twitch muscle fibers effectively while keeping rest periods short enough to maintain an elevated heart rate.
