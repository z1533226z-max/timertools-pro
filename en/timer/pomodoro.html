The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses 25-minute focused work sessions followed by 5-minute breaks.

  1. 25-minute Focus: Concentrate on a single task
  2. 5-minute Break: Short break to recharge
  3. Repeat 4 Sessions: After 4 pomodoros, take a longer break (15 minutes)
  4. Continue: Repeat until daily goals are achieved

üí° Benefits of Pomodoro Technique

  • Enhanced focus and productivity
  • Prevents fatigue accumulation
  • Visualizes work progress
  • Prevents multitasking
  • Reduces burnout risk
