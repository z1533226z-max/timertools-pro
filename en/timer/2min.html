How to Use a 2 Minute Timer

🦷

Teeth Brushing

Dentists recommend brushing for exactly 2 minutes. Spend 30 seconds on each quadrant of your mouth for thorough plaque removal and healthier gums.

🧗

Quick Stretch

A 2-minute stretch routine between work sessions improves blood circulation and reduces muscle tension. Focus on neck, shoulders, and wrists.

🏃

Rest Between Sets

Two minutes is the standard rest period between heavy compound lifts like squats and deadlifts, allowing muscle recovery while maintaining workout intensity.

Why 2 Minutes Is the Perfect Duration

Two minutes (120 seconds) hits a sweet spot for many daily activities. The American Dental Association specifically recommends 2 minutes of brushing to effectively remove plaque. Brushing for less than 120 seconds removes up to 40% less plaque.

In fitness, a 2-minute rest between heavy sets allows your phosphocreatine system to recover 80-90%, providing enough energy for the next set while keeping your heart rate elevated for better conditioning.
