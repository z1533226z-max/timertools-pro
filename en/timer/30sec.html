The Versatility of 30 Seconds

30 seconds strikes the perfect balance between too short and too long. In fitness, a 30-second plank is the gold standard starting point for beginners. Research shows that holding a plank for 30 seconds activates all major core muscles, including the rectus abdominis, obliques, and transverse abdominis.

The 30-second HIIT interval is one of the most studied formats in exercise science. Studies published in the Journal of Sports Medicine demonstrate that 30 seconds of all-out effort followed by rest produces significant improvements in both aerobic and anaerobic fitness, while burning more calories than steady-state cardio.
