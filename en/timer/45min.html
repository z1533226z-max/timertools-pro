How to Use a 45 Minute Timer

🏫

Class Period

The standard school class is 45 minutes - matching the brain's peak attention window. Use this timer for teaching, tutoring, or self-study sessions.

🏋

Full Workout

45 minutes covers a complete gym session: 5-minute warm-up, 35-minute main workout, and 5-minute cool-down. Studies show this is the sweet spot for most fitness goals.

💻

Extended Focus

For tasks requiring deeper concentration than a 25-minute Pomodoro, a 45-minute block gives you uninterrupted time while still preventing burnout.

Why 45 Minutes Works

Forty-five minutes (2,700 seconds) represents three-quarters of an hour and is one of the most common time blocks used in education worldwide. Educational research confirms that student attention peaks around 10-15 minutes into a session and can be sustained with engagement techniques for up to 45-50 minutes.

In fitness, 45 minutes is considered the optimal workout length. After about 45-60 minutes of intense exercise, cortisol (the stress hormone) rises while anabolic hormones decline, making longer sessions potentially counterproductive for muscle building.
