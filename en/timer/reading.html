Reading Session Guide

📖

Quick Read (10-15 min)

Perfect for building a daily habit. Read 5-8 pages of fiction or 1 article. Best for morning routines, commute reading, or before bed. Start here if you are new to daily reading. Consistency matters more than duration.

📚

Standard Session (20-30 min)

The sweet spot for most readers. Read 15-20 pages or one book chapter. Long enough for deep immersion but short enough to maintain focus. Ideal for lunch breaks, evening wind-down, or weekend mornings.

📘

Deep Reading (45-60 min)

For serious study or getting lost in a great book. Read 30-40 pages with full comprehension. Take a 5-minute break halfway to reflect. Best for weekends, holidays, or dedicated reading time. Note-taking recommended for non-fiction.

The Science of Timed Reading

Setting a timer creates a time-bounded focus state. Research from the University of California shows that knowing a session has a defined endpoint reduces mind-wandering by 40%. The timer acts as a commitment device: you are not reading indefinitely, you are reading for exactly 20 minutes.

This is the same principle behind the Pomodoro Technique. Timed reading sessions also help you track progress: if you read 20 minutes daily, that is over 120 hours per year - enough to finish 40-50 books. The timer transforms reading from a vague intention into a measurable habit.
