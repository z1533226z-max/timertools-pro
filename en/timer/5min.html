How to Use a 5 Minute Timer

🧗

5-Minute Meditation

A quick 5-minute mindfulness session can reduce stress and boost focus. It is the ideal duration for beginners to build a daily habit.

🍵

Tea Steeping

Green tea (2-3 min), black tea (3-5 min), herbal tea (5-7 min). Five minutes works perfectly for most tea varieties.

🏃

Pomodoro Break

After 25 minutes of focused work, take a 5-minute break. The Pomodoro Technique relies on these short rest intervals to maintain peak productivity.

What You Can Do in 5 Minutes

Five minutes (300 seconds) is short yet surprisingly productive. You can complete a stretching routine, soft-boil an egg, write a quick journal entry, organize your workspace, or practice deep breathing exercises. It is the perfect length for building small daily habits.

In the Pomodoro Technique, the 5-minute break between 25-minute focus sessions activates your brain's diffuse mode. This brief mental shift restores concentration and dramatically improves productivity in the next session.

Using an online 5 minute timer lets you track time precisely, so your short breaks and tasks stay exactly on schedule. Just click Start and the 300-second countdown begins.
