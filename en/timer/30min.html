The Half-Hour Advantage

Thirty minutes (1,800 seconds) is half an hour and arguably the most versatile time block in daily life. The World Health Organization recommends 30 minutes of moderate physical activity per day for maintaining good health. It is also the standard duration for most fitness classes, therapy sessions, and lunch breaks.

For productivity, 30 minutes is the minimum duration experts recommend for deep work. Cal Newport, author of "Deep Work," suggests that meaningful cognitive tasks require sustained focus of at least 30 minutes to produce quality output.
