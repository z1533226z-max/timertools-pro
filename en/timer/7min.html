The Science Behind 7 Minutes

The 7-minute workout was developed by exercise physiologists and published in the American College of Sports Medicine's journal. Research shows this high-intensity circuit matches the benefits of a prolonged endurance workout in a fraction of the time.

Seven minutes (420 seconds) also appears in cooking: a 7-minute egg produces the most popular jammy consistency served in restaurants worldwide. The timing is precise - even 30 seconds more or less noticeably changes the yolk texture.
