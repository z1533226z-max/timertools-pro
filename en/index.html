Online Timer — Frequently Asked Questions

Is this online timer free to use?

Yes. TimerTools Pro is 100% free with no sign-up required. It runs in any web browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

How do I start the online timer?

Choose a preset such as 5, 10, or 25 minutes, or set a custom time, then press Start. An alarm sounds when the countdown reaches zero.

Does the timer keep running if I switch tabs or lock the screen?

Yes. The timer keeps counting in the background and plays an alert sound when it finishes, even if you switch to another tab.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

It is a time-management method that uses 25-minute focus sessions followed by 5-minute breaks to improve concentration and productivity.

Can I run several timers at the same time?

Yes. The Multi Timer lets you run multiple countdowns at once, which is handy for cooking, workouts, or studying.

Do I need to install an app?

No installation is needed. It works instantly in your browser, and you can optionally add it to your home screen as a PWA.
